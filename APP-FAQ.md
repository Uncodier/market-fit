## Getting Started

### Q: How do I create an account?
**A:** Click on the "Sign Up" button on the login page. You can register using your email address or through social login options if available.

### Q: How do I set up my first site/workspace?
**A:** After registration, you'll be prompted to create your first site. Provide a site name and select your industry. This will be your main workspace.

### Q: Can I have multiple sites?
**A:** Yes, you can create and manage multiple sites from your account. Each site is a separate workspace with its own data and settings.

### Q: How do I invite team members?
**A:** Go to Settings > Team Management and click "Invite Member." Enter their email address and select their role (Admin, Editor, or Viewer).

### Q: What are the different user roles?
**A:** 
- **Admin**: Full access to all features and settings
- **Editor**: Can create, edit, and manage content and campaigns
- **Viewer**: Read-only access to data and reports

## Dashboard

### Q: What information is displayed on the dashboard?
**A:** The dashboard shows key metrics including:
- Revenue and ROI widgets
- Active users and segments
- Recent activity
- Cost reports and sales reports
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Lifetime value (LTV)
- Lead conversion rates

### Q: How do I change the date range for dashboard metrics?
**A:** Use the date range picker in the top section of the dashboard. You can select predefined ranges or set custom dates.

### Q: Can I filter dashboard data by segments?
**A:** Yes, use the segment dropdown to filter dashboard metrics by specific customer segments.

### Q: What are the different dashboard tabs?
**A:** 
- **Overview**: Main metrics and KPIs
- **Segments**: Segment-specific performance data
- **Cohorts**: Customer cohort analysis tables

### Q: How often is dashboard data updated?
**A:** Dashboard data is updated in real-time as new leads, sales, and activities are recorded.

## Leads Management

### Q: How do I create a new lead?
**A:** Click the "+" button in the leads section and fill out the lead form with contact information, source, and other relevant details.

### Q: What are the different lead statuses?
**A:** Lead statuses include:
- **New**: Newly created leads
- **Qualified**: Leads that meet your criteria
- **Contacted**: Leads you've reached out to
- **Converted**: Leads that became customers
- **Lost**: Leads that didn't convert

### Q: How do I track lead journey stages?
**A:** Leads automatically progress through journey stages based on completed tasks:
- Awareness
- Consideration
- Decision
- Purchase
- Retention
- Referral

### Q: Can I import leads from other sources?
**A:** Yes, you can import leads via CSV upload or integrate with other tools through our API.

### Q: How do I segment leads?
**A:** Use the filters in the leads section to create segments based on:
- Lead source
- Status
- Journey stage
- Custom attributes
- Date ranges

### Q: What is lead attribution?
**A:** Lead attribution tracks which campaigns or sources generated each lead, helping you understand your most effective marketing channels.

### Q: How do I view leads in different formats?
**A:** Use the view selector to switch between:
- **Table View**: Detailed list with columns
- **Kanban View**: Card-based workflow view
- **Calendar View**: Timeline-based view

## Control Center (Tasks)

### Q: What is the Control Center?
**A:** The Control Center is your task management hub where you can track, organize, and manage all tasks related to leads, campaigns, and general business activities.

### Q: How do I create a new task?
**A:** Click the "+" button in the Control Center and fill out the task details including title, description, assignee, priority, and due date.

### Q: What are the different task statuses?
**A:** Task statuses include:
- **To Do**: Newly created tasks
- **In Progress**: Tasks currently being worked on
- **Review**: Tasks pending review
- **Done**: Completed tasks

### Q: How do I assign tasks to team members?
**A:** When creating or editing a task, select the assignee from the dropdown list of team members.

### Q: Can I view tasks in different formats?
**A:** Yes, you can switch between:
- **Kanban View**: Board with columns for each status
- **Table View**: Detailed list view
- **Calendar View**: Timeline-based view

### Q: How do I filter tasks?
**A:** Use the filter options to narrow down tasks by:
- Status
- Assignee
- Priority
- Due date
- Task type
- Associated lead

### Q: What is task priority?
**A:** Tasks can be assigned priority levels (High, Medium, Low) to help organize workflow and ensure important tasks are completed first.

## Experiments

### Q: What are Experiments?
**A:** Experiments allow you to run A/B tests on various elements of your marketing campaigns, content, and user experiences.

### Q: How do I create an experiment?
**A:** Go to the Experiments section, click "Create Experiment," and define your test parameters including variants, success metrics, and audience.

### Q: What can I test with experiments?
**A:** You can test:
- Email subject lines
- Landing page designs
- Campaign messaging
- Pricing strategies
- User interface elements

### Q: How long should I run an experiment?
**A:** Run experiments until you achieve statistical significance, typically requiring at least 100-1000 participants depending on your conversion rates.

### Q: How do I interpret experiment results?
**A:** The platform provides statistical analysis including confidence intervals, p-values, and recommendations for which variant performs better.

### Q: Can I stop an experiment early?
**A:** Yes, you can pause or stop experiments at any time, but ensure you have sufficient data for meaningful results.

## Troubleshooting

### Q: Why can't I see my data?
**A:** Check that you're in the correct site/workspace and that you have the appropriate permissions. If the issue persists, try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache.

### Q: The page is loading slowly. What should I do?
**A:** Try refreshing the page, checking your internet connection, or clearing your browser cache. If problems persist, contact support.

### Q: I'm getting error messages. How do I fix them?
**A:** Most error messages include specific instructions. Common solutions include:
- Refreshing the page
- Checking your internet connection
- Ensuring required fields are filled out
- Verifying your permissions

### Q: How do I contact support?
**A:** You can contact support through:
- The help section in the app
- Email support (if available)
- The support chat feature (if enabled)

### Q: Can I recover deleted data?
**A:** Some deleted data may be recoverable within a certain timeframe. Contact support immediately if you've accidentally deleted important information.

### Q: Why am I seeing outdated data?
**A:** Try refreshing the page or checking if you're looking at the correct date range. If data seems consistently outdated, contact support.

### Q: The interface looks broken or elements are missing. What should I do?
**A:** This is often caused by browser compatibility issues or cached data. Try:
- Clearing your browser cache
- Using a different browser
- Disabling browser extensions
- Refreshing the page
